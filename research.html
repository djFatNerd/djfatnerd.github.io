Research Details & Examples

This page provides brief explanations and curated examples for each research direction.

I. Urban Scene Simulation / Generation

Composite pipelines combining generative models, procedural generation and engines to produce diverse and controllable virtual cities.

  • MetaUrban: An Embodied AI Simulation Platform for Urban Micromobility
  • CityDreamer: Compositional Generative Model of Unbounded 3D Cities
  • SemCity: Semantic Scene Generation with Triplane Diffusion
  • BlockFusion: Expandable 3D Scene Generation using Latent Tri-plane Extrapolation

II. Urban Scene Reconstruction

Reconstructing real urban spaces into high-fidelity, actionable environments using NeRF and Gaussian Splatting.

  • Block-NeRF: Scalable Large Scene Neural View Synthesis
  • CityGaussianV2: Efficient and Geometrically Accurate Reconstruction for Large-Scale

III. Urban Scene Understanding

3D understanding, mapping and semantic structure extraction; LLM-assisted planning and reasoning.

  • HUGS: Holistic Urban 3D Scene Understanding via Gaussian Splatting
  • The Cityscapes Dataset for Semantic Urban Scene Understanding

IV. Traffic and Crowd Simulation

Data-driven and agent-based methods for mobility with safety, efficiency, and energy-awareness.

  • Data-Driven Traffic Simulation: A Comprehensive Review
  • Review of Virtual Traffic Simulation and Its Applications

V. Urban Data Analytics

Learning from heterogeneous city data (satellite, street-view, IoT) for forecasting and decision making.

  • VIRL: A Virtual Indoor Reality Lab for Smart City
